Welcome to The Coffee Shop!

At The Coffee Shop We aim to serve the best quality and most interesting varieties of coffees, teas, espresso drinks and accompaniments at all times to everyone. We will provide a pleasant, welcoming environment, and will maintain a clean and attractive coffee house at all times.Enjoy :)

Drink Menu

We strive to treat our customers respectfully as our guests; we intend to offer our guests service that is prompt, efficient, friendly, and relaxed.

Espresso Drinks

Every day we brew our house Colombian and French Roast Coffees as well as a rotating varieties

  1. Espresso
  2. Macchiato – Espresso with a dollop of foam.
  3. Cortado – Espresso with 3oz of warm milk; short and smooth.
  4. Cappuccino – One part espresso, one part milk, one part foam.
  5. Au Lait – Brewed coffee and steamed milk.
